The Lanki Platform
Intelligence for the
family that builds.
A personal platform built to make sense of two things that matter most — money and health. Two operating systems, six engines, one private vault.
Capital OS
Financial intelligence.
Tracks every account, card, debt, investment, and transaction in one place. Turns imported bank statements into structured data — categorized, tracked, and analyzed over time.
Cash Flow Engine
Active
Imports statements from all accounts and cards. AI extracts and categorizes every transaction. Tracks spending by category, merchant, and period. Monitors savings rate, debt payments, recurring costs, and cash flow across months.
Portfolio Engine
Planned
Tracks investments across all platforms — stocks, funds, real estate, and direct positions. Monitors capital deployed, returns, and portfolio allocation over time.
Capital Intelligence
Planned
Reads the full financial picture and surfaces a structured set of observations — spending patterns, debt trajectory, savings gaps, and allocation decisions worth revisiting.
Health OS
Health intelligence.
Keeps a structured health record for each family member — consultations, diagnoses, labs, prescriptions, and vitals — organized and searchable over years, not just recent visits.
Medical Engine
Planned
One record per family member. Every visit, result, medication, and cost — logged by date, doctor, and condition. The kind of record that is useful in an emergency and essential before a specialist.
Biometrics Engine
Planned
Tracks vitals, lab values, and custom metrics over time. Built for manual entry — no wearable required. Visualizes trends across months and years so changes are visible before they become problems.
Health Intelligence
Planned
Reads across the full health record to surface patterns — correlations between metrics, medication timelines, and changes worth discussing at the next appointment.
